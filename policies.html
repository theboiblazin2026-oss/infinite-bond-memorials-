Store Policies

Transparent information about our services and commitment to you

Return & Refund Policy

At Infinite Bond, we understand that the products you order are deeply personal and meaningful. We strive for perfection in every memorial we create.

Personalized Items

Because our products are custom-made with your specific photos and text, we cannot accept returns or exchanges for buyer remorse, incorrect photo selection, or spelling errors made by the customer. Please review all personalization details carefully before submitting your order.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged, broken, or with a clear manufacturing error (such as a printing defect), we will happily provide a free replacement or full refund. To initiate a claim:

  • Contact us within 14 days of delivery.
  • Provide clear photos of the damaged item and the packaging.
  • We will arrange for a replacement to be shipped immediately at no cost to you.

Shipping Policy

All items are printed on demand. Please allow 2-5 business days for production before your item ships.

Estimated Delivery Times

  • Standard Shipping (US): 3-7 business days after production.
  • Expedited Shipping (US): 2-3 business days after production.

You will receive a tracking number via email as soon as your order is dispatched.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This includes your name, contact details, and the personal photos you upload for your memorial products.

Use of Photos

The photos you upload are used strictly for the purpose of creating your product. We do not share, sell, or use your personal photos for marketing purposes without your explicit written consent.

Data Security

Your payment information is processed securely via our payment partners (Stripe/PayPal). We do not store your credit card details on our servers.

Terms of Service

By accessing or using our website, you agree to be bound by these terms. You represent that you have the right and authority to use any photos uploaded for personalization and that they do not infringe on the intellectual property rights of others.

We reserve the right to refuse any order that contains offensive, hate speech, or inappropriate content.
